Answers before you ask

The usual objections.

What if I don't have long-form content to clip?

We'll build a lightweight recording habit together — usually 30–60 minutes a week of you talking on camera or on a podcast, which is more than enough raw material for 60 clips a month. We guide every recording so the content is pre-engineered to clip well.

How do you access my social accounts?

Never by password. We use Meta Business Manager (Instagram + Facebook), TikTok Business Center, and YouTube permissions — they add our email as a specific role. You retain 100% ownership and can revoke access in one click at any time.

How long until I see real results?

Expect directional results in 30 days (clip structure patterns, what hooks your audience), meaningful reach growth by 60–90 days, and compounding return past 90. Short-form is a flywheel — it rewards consistency, not one-off miracles.

Why is it so much cheaper than other agencies?

Because I'm new and I need case studies more than I need margin right now. I'm taking on three founding clients at half-price in exchange for permission to use the results as proof. It's a fair trade: you get professional work at startup pricing, I get the portfolio that lets me charge full rates next year.

What platforms do you post on?

TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts — the three short-form platforms that actually compound. Each clip is formatted and captioned for where it's going: different captions, different hooks, different thumbnail frames. You don't pick one platform; you own all three.

What happens if a clip flops?

Every week's report dissects what worked and what didn't. Flops are data. If a whole month underperforms and we can't diagnose it, you cancel — no hard feelings, no contract lock-in. You earn the retainer every month or we don't keep it.

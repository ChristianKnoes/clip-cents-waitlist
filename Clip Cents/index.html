FAQ

Answers to what course sellers usually ask.

What does a retainer cost? ▾
Standard retainers start at a flat monthly rate and scale with volume (2 vs. 4 clips a day). Founding clients lock in a discounted rate in exchange for a case study. Exact numbers on the discovery call — after we've seen your content.
How fast will I see results? ▾
Expect consistent posting in week one. Meaningful audience growth typically starts showing in weeks 4–8 as the algorithm learns who you're for. This is a compounding channel, not a spike.
Do I need to film new content? ▾
No. If you have webinars, podcasts, YouTube videos, or course lesson footage, that's plenty of raw material. We'll extract clips from what you've already recorded.
Who owns the clips? ▾
You do. Everything we produce is yours, delivered in a shared folder with the source files. If you ever leave, you walk out with every clip we ever made.
Why not just use OpusClip? ▾
AI clippers find “good moments,” not “moments that sell your course.” We script hooks, pick clips that build authority in your specific niche, and test what's actually converting for course sellers this week. You can always run an AI clipper alongside us.
What do I need to get started? ▾
One long-form recording and 30 minutes for a discovery call. That's it. The free sample clip comes first — you don't pay until you've seen the quality.
